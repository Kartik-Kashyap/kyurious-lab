HARMONOGRAPH SIMULATOR

Pendulum Math • Lissajous • Spirograph


Sound Guide:
• Speed: Controls how fast the pattern draws and affects sound pitch/speed
• Damping: Makes the pattern fade naturally (sound volume also fades)
• Trace Mode: Turns off fading → endless drawing + constant sound volume
• Frequencies: Change the base tone of the two oscillators
• Phase: Adds stereo movement / detune to the sound

Turn sound ON and record video to get audio + visuals together!


How Spirograph works:
This simulates two pendulums (X and Y) connected to a pen.
The path is drawn using parametric equations:
x = sin(freqX·t + phase)
y = sin(freqY·t)
with gradual damping to create beautiful decaying patterns.
