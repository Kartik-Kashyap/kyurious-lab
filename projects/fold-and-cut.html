How to use

1. Draw mode (default): • Click on the paper to add vertices. • A preview segment shows from the last vertex to your cursor. 2. When you have ≥ 3 points, click “Close shape”. 3. Switch to “Add fold line”: • Click drag: press at start of fold, release at end. 4. Use “Compute fold line (demo)” to generate a simple central fold axis (not a full theorem-accurate crease pattern). 5. Use the export buttons for PNG / SVG, or print → “Save as PDF”. Note: This is a geometric/UX prototype. A full mathematically exact fold-and-cut algorithm would be the next layer.
